— Hi There! —

I am a fourth-year Business and Computer Science (BUCS) student at UBC Sauder. The BUCS program has allowed me to converge my two greatest passions - business and technology and to subsequently land internships at established companies such as Electronic Arts and Magnitude.



While getting hands-on industry experience, I got the opportunity to sharpen my skills in C++ by writing SDK code for database drivers and developing an ice-hockey game called NHL.

Besides working with C++ full-time, I’ve been exposed to technologies such as Java, JavaScript, Angular, C#, C, Python, Perl, PHP, and various relational and non-relational databases. Furthermore, I am familiar with both agile and traditional project management frameworks, various deployment and testing tools. Besides, I picked up React, HTML, CSS, MERN stack and Swift by working on personal projects (more on my Github).

Aside from academia and programming, I am passionate about snowboarding, ice hockey, and DJing.

I had been a professional snowboarder for 6 years competing on the provincial and national levels in Russia. Snowboarding has significantly shaped me into the individual I am now, and still remains to be a big part of my life. As for ice hockey, I started playing on a junior team when I was 5 years old. Nowadays, I enjoy spending evenings playing hockey in night leagues. On the contrary to the previous two, DJing is something I’ve picked up recently even though music has been my passion for many years. With one year of practice, a friend and I founded an organization called Twocubed and hosted our first event, which more than 100 people attended.

Looking forwards, my goal is to find a full-time job in the software development that will provide me with enough challenge and continuous learning opportunity while at the same time allowing me to pursue things that matter to me outside of working hours.

If you are interested in learning more about me or sharing something about yourself, I would be happy to chat. So let’s connect!
