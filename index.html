— About Me —

Hey! Nice seeing you on my page! I am a third year BUCS student at UBC Sauder. Two years ago I came to Vancouver, Canada from Russia to pursue my academic and career goals. Make sure to check them out on my GitHub! My interest lies in web and mobile development, primarily back-end. Whenever I don't code, you can find me playing ice-hockey or snowboarding.
